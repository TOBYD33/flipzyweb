# **Flipzy Waitlist Landing Page PRD (Codex Version)**

## **Product**

Flipzy Website – Waitlist Landing Page

## **Page Type**

Single-page landing page designed to:

• Explain Flipzy quickly  
 • Capture emails for early access  
 • Build hype before the mobile app launch  
 • Direct users to future **iOS and Android downloads**

---

# **1\. Product Vision**

Flipzy is a **social discovery \+ marketplace app** where people can:

• Meet new people  
 • Network and collaborate  
 • Discover services  
 • Buy and sell products  
 • Build communities

All through a **swipe-first mobile experience**.

Think:

**Bumble \+ Jiji \+ Gen Z social energy**

But built for:

• students  
 • creators  
 • hustlers  
 • young professionals  
 • communities worldwide
